Overview

Online monetary transactions are associated with a high risk of cybersecurity threats. With the combination of our 4 distinct ID Verification Services, we help make your digitization process frictionless and risk-free.

KYC Documents

Our AML/KYC procedure includes confirming the identity of active users with high trading volume, therefore, you are required to provide the following:

Document VerificationA valid high-quality photo of your ID (passport or driver license)
Data ExtractionUtility bill for proof of address.
Face Verification A picture of you holding your ID card.
OptionalProof of origin of funds;

Once you have the whole documents ready for submission, please send them to our email, security@Vitron-trade.com, with the subject, "KYC Verification".
